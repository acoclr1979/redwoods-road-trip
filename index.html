🗺️ Itinerary

July 29 – August 2, 2026

Wed, Jul 29
SF Arrival Day SF Plans — TBD

Land at SFO 10:30am on flight 261, cross the Golden Gate, and pick up the rental car. Still deciding how to spend the day in San Francisco before heading north — a local gym session, the Marina, or Alcatraz, maybe with an In-N-Out stop on the way out of the city. Check in to Hotel Trio Healdsburg between 6–8pm.

Thu, Jul 30
Drive North

Depart Healdsburg — morning and afternoon are cool scenic drives through the Avenue of the Giants, 31 miles through ancient redwoods. Check in to Sanderling Cabin, Moonstone Beach after 4pm, then catch the sunset that evening.

Fri, Jul 31
Tall Trees Grove Permit Secured

Tall Trees Grove permit hike in the morning — among some of the tallest trees on Earth. Grab lunch on the way back, then head to the condo/beach area at Moonstone Beach for the afternoon.

Sat, Aug 1
Coastline & Trinidad Head Fern Canyon — Maybe

Coastline exploring and Trinidad Head. Fern Canyon is the stretch goal for the day — no permit secured yet, so it's a walk-up-availability long shot.

Sun, Aug 2
Drive Back to SFO

5:00am departure from Sanderling Cabin — it's a ~5h45m drive to SFO. Return the rental car, then flight 2518 departs SFO at 1:10pm.
